Sponsorship

After three successful editions from 2011 until 2013 we continue our work of establishing the Frontend Conference as a leading conference about state-of-the-art frontend and UX technologies in Europe and Switzerland.

Benefit Packages

All sponsors except Silver can book the following additions:

  • Lanyards (CHF 1'000.–)
  • Presentation table in the main hall or lounge (CHF 500.–)
  • Flyers in lounge (CHF 250.–)
  • Rollup Display (CHF 250.–)

Standalone Packages

Additionally we are looking for sponsors in the following areas:

  • Official Party (CHF 5'000.–)
  • Lunch incl. drinks (CHF 7'500 for both days/CHF 4'000.– for one day, sponsor logo will be printed on the food vouchers)

  • Coffee in the lounge (CHF 2'000.-)
